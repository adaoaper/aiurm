# AIURM Protocol
AIURM/AIUAR Context Space Workflow Specification
DLR Methodology

# Workflow
Credit Risk Assessment (v0.1)
Multi-Session Worker Pattern - One Proposal per Session

# Purpose
Demonstrate AIURM/AIUAR with a credit risk pipeline operating as a queue-driven worker:
a middleware writes one normalized loan application per session; an LLM worker claims
pending sessions (those without any *result_* marker) and resolves them sequentially.
Logic and policy parameters are anchored in session_1 and resolved via cross-session reference.
