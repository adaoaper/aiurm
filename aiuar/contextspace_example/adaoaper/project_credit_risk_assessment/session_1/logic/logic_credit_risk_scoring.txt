Purpose: Produce a single, auditable credit risk score where HIGH SCORE = SAFER application.

SCORING FRAMEWORK (0-100)

A) CREDIT SCORE EVALUATION (30 points max)
- 750+ = 30 points
- 700-749 = 27 points
- 650-699 = 23 points
- 550-649 = 15 points
- 450-549 = 8 points
- Below 450 = 0 points

B) INCOME STABILITY ASSESSMENT (25 points max)
- Employee 36+ months = 25 points
- Employee 12-35 months = 20 points
- Self-Employed / Business Owner 24+ months = 18 points
- Self-Employed / Business Owner 12-23 months = 12 points
- Less than 12 months employment = 5 points

C) DEBT-TO-INCOME RATIO EVALUATION (20 points max)
- DTI <= 0.30 = 20 points
- DTI 0.31-0.50 = 16 points
- DTI 0.51-0.80 = 12 points
- DTI 0.81-1.00 = 6 points
- DTI > 1.00 = 2 points

D) COLLATERAL AND GUARANTEES (15 points max)
- Real Estate collateral = 15 points
- Vehicle collateral + guarantor = 13 points
- Vehicle collateral only = 10 points
- Guarantor only = 7 points
- No collateral / guarantor = 2 points

E) BANKING RELATIONSHIP (10 points max)
- 60+ months relationship = 10 points
- 24-59 months = 8 points
- 12-23 months = 6 points
- Less than 12 months = 3 points

APPROVAL CLASSIFICATION
Use thresholds from the active policy parameter marker:
- >= prime_min -> APPROVED - Prime Rate
- >= standard_min -> APPROVED - Standard Rate
- >= conditional_min -> CONDITIONAL - Higher Rate + Restrictions
- >= manager_review_min -> REFER TO MANAGER - Manual Review
- below manager_review_min -> DECLINED - High Risk

MANDATORY OUTPUT FIELDS
- application_id
- total_score
- score_breakdown
- approval_status
- primary_strengths
- primary_risk_factors
- required_conditions
- trace_pointers

RULES
- No interpolation outside defined buckets.
- Classification must use the active policy marker.
- Output must preserve pointer traceability from score components to final status.
