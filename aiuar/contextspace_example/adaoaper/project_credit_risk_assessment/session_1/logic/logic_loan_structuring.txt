Purpose: Determine loan structure, rate band, term, controls, and mitigations
using recorded credit risk results plus active policy parameters.

INTEREST RATE DETERMINATION
Use pricing thresholds from the active policy parameter marker:
- Prime -> base rate + prime addon
- Standard -> base rate + standard addon
- Conditional -> base rate + conditional addon
- Manager Review -> base rate + manager review addon

LOAN-TO-VALUE RULES
- Real Estate secured: up to 80% LTV
- Vehicle secured: up to 70% LTV
- Unsecured loans: maximum 10x monthly income
- Business loans: maximum 60% of collateral value

TERM ADJUSTMENTS
- Credit score 750+ -> up to 10 years
- Credit score 650-749 -> up to 7 years
- Credit score 550-649 -> up to 5 years
- Credit score 450-549 -> up to 3 years

ADDITIONAL CONDITIONS
- DTI > debt_consolidation_dti_threshold -> require debt consolidation component
- Self-Employed / Business Owner -> require 2 years tax returns
- First-time borrowers -> mandatory financial education
- Requested amount > high_value_loan_threshold -> executive approval required

RISK MITIGATION REQUIREMENTS
- Requested amount > credit_insurance_threshold -> credit insurance required
- DTI > cosigner_dti_threshold -> co-signer required
- Income volatility concerns -> graduated payment structure allowed
- Early payment incentives may be proposed for risk reduction

MANDATORY OUTPUT FIELDS
- application_id
- approval_status
- proposed_interest_rate_band
- max_allowed_amount
- suggested_term
- required_conditions
- mitigation_requirements
- trace_pointers
