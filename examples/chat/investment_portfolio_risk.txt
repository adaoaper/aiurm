L1. Portfolio Risk Scoring Criteria (Enterprise • Corrected • Strict Buckets)
==========================================================
Purpose: Produce a single, auditable risk score where HIGH SCORE = SAFER portfolio.
This logic is strictly discrete for bucketed components (NO interpolation).

BUCKET ENFORCEMENT RULE (CRITICAL)
- The following components MUST use ONLY the discrete bucket values listed below:
  Diversification, Sector Concentration, Liquidity, Risk-Profile Alignment.
- NO interpolation, smoothing, or fractional bucket values are allowed.
- If a raw metric falls between two bucket thresholds, apply:
  TIE-BREAKER = FLOOR_TO_RISKIER_BUCKET
  (i.e., choose the lower / riskier bucket unless the higher bucket is strictly satisfied).
- Any output not matching an allowed bucket value is a POLICY VIOLATION and must be recomputed discretely.

SCORING FRAMEWORK (0–100)

A) ASSET CLASS STABILITY (30% weight)  [continuous via value-weighted average]
Stability points per holding:
- Government bonds = 30 points (Very Low Risk)
- Investment-grade / high-quality corporate bonds (AAA–A / IG) = 25 points
- Diversified broad-market ETFs = 20 points
- Blue-chip equities = 15 points
- Growth Tech / Innovation equities or ETFs = 10 points
- Crypto assets = 5 points (Very High Risk)

Taxonomy override:
- Any asset listed in *asset_taxonomy.crypto_proxies (e.g., MSTR, COIN) MUST be treated as Crypto = 5 points.
- Any asset listed in *asset_taxonomy.growth_tech_proxies is treated as Growth Tech = 10 points.

Aggregation rule:
- AssetStability_raw = Σ(value_weight_i × stability_points_i)
- Asset Stability contribution to final score = AssetStability_raw  (max 30)

B) DIVERSIFICATION SCORE (25% weight)  [STRICT DISCRETE BUCKETS]
Count distinct asset classes in the portfolio (e.g., Equity, Bond, ETF, Crypto, Commodity, Real Estate, Cash, Alternatives):
- ≥ 5 asset classes = 25 points
- 3–4 asset classes = 18 points
- 2 asset classes = 12 points
- 1 asset class only = 5 points

C) SECTOR CONCENTRATION (20% weight)  [STRICT DISCRETE BUCKETS]
Compute % of total portfolio value in the single largest sector (after taxonomy overrides):
- < 30% concentration = 20 points
- 30–49% concentration = 12 points
- ≥ 50% in one sector = 5 points

D) LIQUIDITY ASSESSMENT (15% weight)  [STRICT DISCRETE BUCKETS]
Compute % of portfolio in liquid assets (ETFs, blue-chip equities, government bonds, IG bonds):
- ≥ 70% liquid assets = 15 points
- 40–69% liquid assets = 10 points
- < 40% liquid assets = 5 points

Notes:
- Crypto and crypto-proxy equities are treated as liquidity-risk assets (not counted as liquid for this bucket).

E) RISK PROFILE ALIGNMENT (10% weight)  [STRICT DISCRETE BUCKETS]
Assess suitability between investor profile and holdings risk posture:
- Holdings consistent with declared profile = 10 points
- Moderate mismatch = 6 points
- Severe mismatch = 2 points

Guidance:
- Conservative portfolios with >10% Crypto/crypto-proxy exposure → Severe mismatch.
- Balanced portfolios with >25% Growth Tech / Innovation or >20% Crypto → Moderate to Severe mismatch.
- Aggressive portfolios may tolerate higher volatility, but extreme hidden-beta or concentration can still produce mismatch.

FINAL THRESHOLDS (unchanged)
- 80–100 points = "Low Risk Portfolio"
- 65–79 points  = "Moderate Risk Portfolio"
- 50–64 points  = "Elevated Risk Portfolio"
- Below 50      = "High Risk Portfolio"
